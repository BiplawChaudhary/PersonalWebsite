Being Introvert

9 July 2021 | Biplaw

Introvert, you might be familiar with this term. For those who don't know what an introvert is, "An introvert is a person with qualities of a personality type known as introversion, which means that they feel more comfortable focusing on their inner thoughts and ideas, rather than what's happening externally. They enjoy spending time with just one or two people, rather than large groups or crowds."
And I am one of those people.
